What is Pop It exactly?

This is a child's game, in which the process can be compared to teh bursting of a bubble wrap. But unlike film, pop-it can be pressed with the same characteristic sensations and sound "endlessly", dozens of silicone bubble are simply squeezed out in the other direction.

Choose your Pop-It!

Rules of the game

Players take turns and click on the bumps. Ir is imperative to finish the row that you started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at the same time (they must be near, you cannot press several pimples at once, if they are not close to each other). The task is to make the opponent go last. Who went last - lost!
